Metroid Dread

Developer: Mercury Steam Entertainment

ESRB Rating: T Teen

Thought to be long forgotten, Metroid Dread is the fifth installment in the mainline Metroid series. Initially announced back in 2005 as the sequel to Metroid Fusion (2002) on the Gameboy advance. A return to the side-scrolling gameplay of previous Metroid games and all new stealth elements, this is the first original 2D Metroid game in 19 years and a continuation of the story that began in 1986!

After video evidence sent to the Galactic Federation reveals that X parasites - thought to be extinct after their native planet's destruction - might still be alive on the remote planet ZDR, seven EMMI (Extraplanetary Multiform Mobile Identifier) robots are sent there to investigate. Contact is quickly lost with the EMMIs, and so the Federation sends the X-immune Samus Aran to take their stead.
