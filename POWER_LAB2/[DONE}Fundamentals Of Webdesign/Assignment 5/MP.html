Meatball Pizza

- Serving: 4-6
- Calories: 370
Prep/Cook Time: 20mins -

---- Ingredients
  • 22 frozen meatballs
  • 1 12-14 inch pizza crusts
  • 1 14 ounce jar pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
Instructions ----

preheat oven 500F degrees.
Lightly grease a pizza pan with oil, sprinkle about 2 tablespoons flour on the pan.
Place pizza crust on ungreased pizza pan. Spread sauce evenly over crust.
Now add add the meatballs, layering evenly. Sprinkle cheese
Bake until cheese is melted and bubbly and the crust is golden, about 20 minutes.
